Welcome to my portfolio!

My name is Vania, and I am a Graphic Design student. Here you will find my best works or projects. I mainly work with Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign; however, I also use Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects.

A little about myself

I am 21 years old, and I am currently pursuing my Associates degree in Graphic Design. I was born and raised in Lima, Peru; however, I moved to the United States in 2018. I am passionate about print design and digital design, and I often spend my time creating posters for my favorite TV shows, movies or t-shirts inspired by my favorite bands. During my free time, I like to listen to music, play with my dog Gary, experiment with Photoshop and Illustrator, play the guitar, and watch movies and TV shows.
